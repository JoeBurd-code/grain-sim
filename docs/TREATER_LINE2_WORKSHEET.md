## Line-wide questions

These are not about a single machine. They cover how the line behaves as a whole.

- Interlocks: when a bin reaches a high level, or a machine trips, which upstream
  equipment stops, in what order, and after how long? Is there a cause-and-effect list
  we can have?
- Conveyor ramp times: how long does each variable-speed conveyor take to speed up and
  slow down? Which equipment stops almost instantly (direct-on-line)?
- Control response: is there a typical delay between a sensor reading and the equipment
  reacting (controller scan, network) that is worth allowing for?
- High-high level switches: where are these actually fitted along the line?
- Start-up sequence: how long does the siren sound before motion, and in what order does
  the line start up?
- Routing: at the top conveyor, what decides which of the three branches runs?
- Where removed metal, samples, and rejects go, for each take-off point along the line.
- Where the outload metal bins discharge (truck loadout), and how that is controlled.
